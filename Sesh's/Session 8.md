**Mexican Stand Off with Big Wolf-Lion "Spirit"**

We start this session were we left off at the Ethe'Auris shrine, in the Auflen forest, in a stand off with the "Spirit", combat mode is engaged.

One of the spirit's eyes appears to be that of a wolf, the other of a lion. 40-50% of this creature is very lion-esque. There are some open wounds and scars all over this thing

Virk steps back 5ft, away from the beast and off of the raised platform. He then gets ready to throw is axe at the creature.

Remy also steps back away from the creature.

Brodie steps up to the creature and tries to seduce it. He changes is eyes to be the most dazzling eyes ever seen. He cannot seduce it but, with Persuasion (18) and Animal Handling (22), he presents himself as non-threatening and communicates with body-language that he is not going to hurt it.

**Here Comes the Wolves (do-do-do-doo)**

Four wolves dash from the tree-line towards the creature, they pounce on it, and immediately start gnawing and biting chunks of flesh. 

The "Spirit" turns around and swipes once and rips the head off one of the wolves. It then goes to bite the head off another but only manages to graze the side of it.

Fenwick rushes over to the creatures aid and smacks one wolf down to the ground (knocking it out).

Khamoraz runs in between the creature and the remaining two wolves and says "Stop fighting" (not exactly that but something similar). He then swings at one of the wolves with his great sword and kills it immediately. He then looks towards the other one and says "Back off!". The last wolf seems unbothered by Khamoraz.

Azaia moves to the right of the wooden decking and prepares a firebolt in case anyone approaches.

This creature has recognised we are fighting for it. There was a big chunk taken out of the lion-like area. That area starts to regress sightly and the wolf part starts to expand in that area.

Azaia notices there are more sounds coming from the forest and sees 2 wolves by the tree-line bounding over.

Virk is confused. He decides to reflect back on his military career to try and foresee the outcome of this encounter. After Dr Strange-ing to calculate all 242 billion outcomes, he concludes this chimera creature is a wild-card (so wise). He also surmises that there are probably going to be more wolves coming for this creature. 

After coming up with this valuable wisdom he moves towards the trees and just chills out there, not wanting to get involved right now. He notices two wolves and hears the rumblings of potentially some larger creature.

Geoffrey, with the help of Remy, manages to get up onto the roof this time. The roof is slanted and so it is difficult to manoeuvre while on it. He shoots at one of the wolves in the tree line and the arrow flies right through its neck and the wolf bleeds out and dies.

Brodie slides on his knees between the chimera creature's legs to get between it and the wolf that is nearest to it. He uses his newly obtained cool dagger and slashes at the wolf. The wolf then bites Brodie.

Wolf in the tree line rushes towards Fenwick and tries to bite him but he dodges it.

Two dire wolves emerge from the forest. One rushes to Fenwick and gnashes at his right leg. 

The chimera charges at the Dire wolf that attacked Fenwick and rips a chunk out of its neck.

Fenwick slaps the smaller wolf that tried to bite him. *He then tries to run away but the Dire wolf and small wolf snap at his legs and knock him down.* This was later retconned.

Khamoraz tells the tree spirit to run away so that it does not die. He then charges towards the second dire wolf and slam his great sword into it.

It's rewind time! Azaia's readied firebolt was activated when the first dire wolf approached Fenwick. She successfully hits the dire wolf meaning that when the chimera attacked it actually, killed it. Fenwick is now not dead.

Azaia now sends another firebolt at the other wolf by Fenwick and incinerates it.

Virk runs towards Geoffrey to talk with him. It's rewind time! Virk does not run towards Geoffrey. Virk is not a wood expert. Virk starts shouting at Geoffrey.

Virk: Geoffrey, what's the plan here? Are we going to continue attacking, are more going to come. Do we think having all these people around this creature is wise?

Geoffrey: We are managing the wolves so just mop them up.

Virk then rushes towards Brodie's aid and attempts to stab at the wolf by him but misses. He tries again and this time slashes at the wolves neck and blood gushes out and it has been slain. Virk did something, yay!

Remy rushes to Azaia and hides behind her.

Geoffrey shoots at the last Dire wolf and it lands an arrow in its belly. The beast appears to be heavily injured now (on death's door).

Brodie uses "Shatter" to hit the remaining dire wolf and 2 regular wolves. He pulls out his bagpipes but the sound comes out from the spells centre and the volume of the sound is so loud and booming that it induces heart attacks in the remaining wolves. They all collapse and die. Brodie then clambers on top of the dire wolf to demonstrate he has come out victorious in the battle. This was an epic moment.

Azaia notices with her line of sight of the "spirit" that, as the spell goes off, the wolf parts of the creature regress. The lion eye then appears to be grinning. The lion parts of the creature begin to expand.

The lion roars really loud. Khamoraz, Brodie, Geoffrey, Virk and Remy feel compelled to kneel. They kneel with one fist on the ground (superhero pose). Mechanically they are restrained/prone. 

Geoffrey, in the process, falls from the roof directly onto his bottom. Ouch.

The "spirit" hears the clambering of Geoffrey falling. It sees Fenwick didn't kneel and bites him. It then moves over to Geoffrey, seeing that he failed to kneel, and sinks its fangs into his arm.

Brodie notices that the red aura around the creature has expanded. He also notices a green aura around the tree. He also notices specks of green in the aura of the creature that he thinks are linked to the wolf part of the creature.

Fenwick yells and tries to reason with it. When we first met the creature it seemed quite scared. Now, Fenwick notices that the scaredness has dissipated and has been replaced with anger and a sense of pride.

Fenwick gets the feeling that some of the actions we have taken has helped the condition of this creature. But the creature is still conflicted (possibly Jekyll and Hyde situation). Fenwick clambers onto the roof and moves close to where the creature is.

Khamoraz gets the feeling that if he can damage certain parts of the creature that will help the other parts of the creature. Khamoraz attempts to throw a javelin at the creature but it flies just past it.

Azaia feels that if she can eliminate the foreign parts (lion part) of the creature the innate parts will have an easier time overcoming them. She tries to send a "Witch Bolt" at the creature but misses. Azaia also feels that the magic feeling from this creature seems quite "wild" and "untameable".

Virk is still kneeling. He tries to stand up. As he tries he sees flashes of his military career where he hasn't agreed the orders he has been given but has had to obey them.

Remy fails to stand up.

Geoffrey successfully breaks free from the restraints. He also has flashes of his military career before his eyes. 

He gets up and looks the creature straight in the eye. The wolf eye looks scared and the lion eye looks furious. 

Geoffrey then proceeds to kneel down again to demonstrate that he is kneeling not just out of necessity but out of choice (thinking this his only chance at survival). 

Brodie breaks free of the kneeling and casts bardic inspiration on Fenwick.

Fenwick darts then leaps onto the creature's head from the roof. He smacks the creature in the head a few times before pushing off of it back down onto the ground.

The creature goes to attack Geoffrey, he uses his readied attack to thrust his shortsword at the creature and knock it prone. The creature gets back up and bites Geoffrey twice. Geoffrey is knocked down

Khamoraz breaks free and has flashes of the mountains and his community. He feels a sense of great pride and begins to rage. Khamoraz slashes at the right hand side of the creature (the lion side).

Khamoraz notices that the tree doesn't look healthy. Like a tree that hasn't had enough nutrients (baron land around it).  As Khamoraz steps over some of the roots he thinks about how he would expect these roots to be underground. He also notices how the roots were moving to allow him to step around. Khamoraz also tries to yell at the Chimera to intimidate it.

Azaia casts witchbolt at the Chiemera and hits it.

Khamoraz knows there is a moment where when two mages of a similar school battle. The is a moment of harmony (similar wavelength). He feels that the magic involved here feels "samey".

We now all notice that the roots have now moved a lot and have tried to usher Geoffrey out and have started entangling the creature. There is a flow of energy down form the tree into its roots. The nature of this attack feels familiar.

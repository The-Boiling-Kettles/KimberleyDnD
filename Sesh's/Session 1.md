We begin at [[Training Grounds]] where we undergo a combat test ordered my some fancy elf Noble in shiny armour.

We successfully complete the test as a group by defeating some animals (black bears, boars, wolves).

[[Commander Aedrik Thalor]] is pleased with our performance but concerned (about what we might get asked to do).

Fancy elf Noble from "front line" duchy is pleased with himself after our performance.

After short 50-min break, admin introduces us to half-elf [[Oric Halloway]].
He also us that the city (and training grounds) send people (like Oric) to collect grain and whatnot from local towns.

The next day we are informed by Oric that the person meant to collect grain from [[Harufen]] (a local village) did not return.

As we approach, we notice that the village is very barren and, the people that are around, don't seem to be having much fun and don't seem to be well fed. 

We get to village hall and speak with an [[Elder]] of the village about the missing grain collection. He tells us that the previous collector left and went south with some of their grain and didn't return. [[Azaia]] glares at him and says "Tell us the truth" and the Elder breaks down and says that there have been complications with their harvest and the previous collector was asking for more than they could give. This resulted in "complications" in the barn and the Elder won't answer further questions and instead directs us to [[Caldus]], the priest of the local temple.

We go to the temple and speak with [[Caldus]] who tells us they have had some problems with some guardian spirits they put in the barn attacking people. He also informs us that because of this he has put seals on the barn door.

We go with [[Caldus]] to the barn. We convince him to "Release the seals!" and also convince him to walk into the barn with us.

After a fight with some little twig creatures and big vine dude, we conclude that we have rid the barn of the spirits. We also find remnants of a cart and bones (from previous grain collector).

We convince Oric that we will only take the emergency supply of grain (1/4 of regular grain collection). We also go to inform the Elder that the spirits are gone and how much grain we're taking.

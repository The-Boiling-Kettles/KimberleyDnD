We were informed by [[Commander Aedrik Thalor]] that multiple cargo runs would be taking place from the [[Training Grounds]] to the front lines. Some of these convoys would be decoys not actually containing cargo and while this was kept hidden from the transporters themselves, we were somewhat lead to believe we would be a decoy.

Upon setting off, Khamoraz almost immediately tries to get into the crates but fails. 

On the main road we travel down we come across a fallen down tree. It is here we come across a sketchy looking guy who, after investigating a bit, we find to be some kind of illegal steel merchant.

After leaving this guy we decide to pull over for the night to stay at [[Session 1.5 Tavern]]. We book a room for each of us (except Khamoraz who decides to sleep in stables) and Brodie books a double. Brodie seduces one of the jolly men in shoddy armour, [[Brodie Body 1]], and takes him to bed despite the bar man indicating to Geoffrey that the men are shifty (potentially anti-military).

Overnight, Khamoraz breaks into the crates to discover that the cargo they are carrying actually does contain goods. 

In the morning Azaia has fallen ill. Geoffrey also pieces together that Khamoraz has broken into the crates and warns him of his defiance.

We continue on our journey and after travelling for an hour or so into some forest, we run into some guys in shoddy armour. These men claim to be good guys but are highly suspicious and after not long, combat ensues. 

We manage to just about fight off all the enemies (even though some of them ambushed us with bows and arrows from the sides). [[Oric Halloway]] fights particularly valiantly before being downed right at the end (still MVP though).

We then make the final leg of the trip to the [[Camp Oakenfield]] (rocking up with a cart that looks like a hedgehog from all of the arrows in it). We hang out at the camp for a bit before heading back "home" to the Training Grounds.

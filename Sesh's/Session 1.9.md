We start of by waking up in the night in the [[Training Grounds]] to the sounds of a great commotion going on outside. Once we get outside of our barracks we see that the Training Grounds are under attack. We see a figure dressed in a black, [[Mysterious Figure]] ,running away from the scene and, despite our best efforts, we lose him in [[Auflen Forest]].

In the morning, [[Commander Aedrik Thalor]] gives us the task of finding out more about last night's attack.

We group up (retrieving Brodie from his pleasant night with [[Brodie Body 2]]) and head towards the Auflen Forest where we lost [[Mysterious Figure]]. 

Khamoraz confesses to the group about breaking into the crate of goods from before and reveals that he saw a map. Using Khamoraz's memory of the map we make our way to [[North Bandit Camp]]. Before reaching the camp, we disguise ourselves as bandits with the help of Brodie.

We successfully convince the first set of guards at the gates of the Bandit Camp that we too are bandits. However, as we continue further into the camp, we meet a Bandit Captain that is suspicious of us.

The Bandit Captain leads Geoffrey and Khamoraz up towards some tents.

Then the Bandits attack us and major combat ensues.

Brodie begins by trying to run away but runs straight into more bandits and is quickly downed.

Azaia is also left alone and is quickly cornered and downed by a bandit. But just before she is downed she sends a fireball that starts a fire in the surrounding forest.

Geoffrey and Khamoraz successfully overpower the bandits that initially ambushed them.

Khamoraz leaps from a cliff onto wooden watchtower and splits a bandit clean in half in one epic swoop.

Fenwick finds himself chasing after a singular bandit captain ([[Remy Grin]]) that tries to run away. Fenwick successfully convinces the bandit to stop running away and immobilises him with fear.

Geoffrey ends up getting downed late in the battle.

Once all the bandits are captured or killed, we are left with the forest with 2 downed team members (Geoffrey and Azaia) and 1 team member slain (Brodie R.I.P).
